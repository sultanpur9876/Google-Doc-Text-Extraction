Salary/Stipends

Salary Payment:

Salaries/stipends are generally paid by the 7th of the month for work performed the preceding month. Employees will be notified if payment is delayed. Unless specified otherwise, salaries/stipends are transferred automatically to the employee’s account. Please note, bank processing times may vary.

Confidentiality:

Salary information is strictly confidential. Employees must not disclose their salary to anyone, including other employees or HR. Sharing salary information can lead to confusion and distrust. Employees may seek guidance from theFinance Adminregarding tax savings or pay slips.

Questions About Salary:

For questions regarding salary payment, breakdown, TDS, or benefits, employees should consult the Finance Admin, or refer to their payslips. Other salary inquiries should be directed to theDirector.

Zoho Payroll Portal:

Employees can access the Zoho Payroll Portal to:

  • Review personal information (UAN, ESIC, Bank Account Number)
  • Access pay slips, salary statements, Form 16, and other reports
  • View deductions and tax-saving options
  • Run reports on tax computation under different regimes
  • Upload investment details, rent receipts, and other necessary documents
  • For assistance with updating investments or any portal-related queries, employees can consult the Finance Admin.

Salary Increments

We review employee performance biannually in January/July and April/October for potential salary adjustments. Note that not all reviews result in salary changes. Adjustments are based on past, present, and anticipated future performance, considering various factors:

Company Performance & Strategy:

  • Overall company sales and profits in the past six months
  • Budget and projected cash flows
  • Marketing strategy and technology roadmap

Employee Performance:

  • Competency and Responsibilities:
  • Fulfilled role responsibilities
  • Quality of work
  • Participation in internal and external projects
  • Billable hours
  • Communication:
  • Effective and timely communication
  • Active listening
  • Attitude:
  • Commitment to organizational values and vision
  • Collaborative and cooperative behavior
  • Handling and providing feedback appropriately
  • Emotional control and respectful responses
  • Flexibility and adaptability
  • Participation in development activities and workshops
  • Positive attitude towards others’ growth
  • Giving credit to others for their contributions
  • Self-Organization:
  • Adherence to company processes and guidelines
  • Organizational Contribution:
  • Enhancing organizational culture
  • Mentoring colleagues
  • Assisting others with tasks and projects
  • Suggesting improvements for the company
  • Skill Development:
  • Learning new skills beneficial to self and the organization
  • Continuous learning relevant to the role
  • Ownership and Initiative:
  • Taking responsibility for tasks
  • Proactively initiating new tasks
  • Team Care:
  • Supporting and developing other employees
  • Maintaining team morale
  • Title Change:
  • Any title change in the last six months
  • Salary Adjustment for Intro Period Completion

Salary Adjustment for Intro Period completion

After the Intro Period, salary increments reflected on the payslip after one full calendar month for review. For example, if completed on January 15th, the new salary is reflected in March's payslip. Increments are not prorated.

Bonus

Rubico awards bonuses based on compliance and performance. Bonus amounts vary for each individual and are confidential.

Annual Bonus:

Considered with December’s pay, based on the company's past and anticipated performance.

Eligibility requirements:

Active full time employee in good standing

Not on a notice period

Bonus may be prorated for certain circumstances also (maternity leave, unpaid leave, active only part of the year, etc).

Other Bonuses:

When applicable, monthly bonuses are calculated and displayed on the employee’s payslip.

Loan for Emergency Situation

In the event of a personalemergency/crisiswhich necessitates the need for immediate money, Rubico may consider granting a small loan with permission from theDirector. This loan must be paid back per agreed upon terms.
